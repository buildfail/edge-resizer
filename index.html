🔳 Edge Resizer

What is Edge-Resizer?

🛠️ On-Demand

Forget about keeping thumbnails or batch generating variants. They'll be created on-the-fly

🚀 Edge Cached

After the first request for a given image/variation, further ones will answer from the edge cache

🔗 Your own, compact URLs

Compact URLs, no searchParams, no urldecoding. Just your own domain. (Or ours, we're ok with that)
